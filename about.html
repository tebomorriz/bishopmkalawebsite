    • About BMA

    At Bishop Mkala Academy, we are committed to providing a holistic education that
    nurtures both academic and moral excellence. programs are designed to develop
    learners who are confident, creative, and critical thinkers.

    CBE & CBC Curriculum

    We offer a Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) that encourages learners to apply knowledge in real-life situations.

    Learner-Friendly Environment

    Our classrooms and play areas are designed to be safe, engaging, and inclusive for all students.

    Co-curricular Activities

    We believe in developing the whole child through sports, creativity, and cultural programs.

    Certified Teachers

    Our dedicated and professional teachers ensure that every learner achieves their full potential.
